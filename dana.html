We met Horn in 2009 when he was our tuk-tuk driver. He's kind & funny, & became a good friend. Despite serious lung disease, Horn works hard to support his wife and 3 daughters. But life if precarious in Cambodia & one bad illness in the family can be a tipping point.

We have helped support Horn through rough patches, including last year when he was hospitalized in July & again in August, when he almost died. This month he was hospitalized a third time and had to go to Thailand for care.

Horn is a fighter, but now he can only survive tethered to an oxygen tank. He owes the hospital $3500, in addition to continuing costs for oxygen. We are asking for your help to cover costs. Whether you give $20 or $200, you will be making a direct impact on someone's life and well-being.

Katrina, Tadashi, Horn & his wife Darany thank you from the bottom of our hearts!

"We really would like to thank you very much for your kindness to us, we wish you success and all the best!" - Horn
